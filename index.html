What we do

Our ethos is simple: knowledge and impact are intertwined.
All of our products and services described below are provided in exchange for donations to charities around the world.
If you are a charity that wishes to partner with us, please do contact us and we will see what we can do for you!

All proceeds go to Charity

Our main purpose is to help aspiring consultants break into the industry, so we do not charge anything for event attendance. All of our "premium" products are offered in exchange for direct charity donations. We do not directly accept any money from our event attendees - all proceeds are processed through the charities' official portals/donation systems.

Case Interview/Skills Webinars

We aim to cover a full range of topics with an emphasis on advanced techniques and examples. Most of the free corporate workshops only cover the basics and other premium resources are too expensive for most students and entry-level candidates. Thus, our workshops and webinars are a great way to increases your chances at getting a job offer while keeping to a tight budget.

Mock Interviews

During and after our events we often offer 1:1 mock interviews in exchange for a modest donation to one of the charities of our choice. These sessions are 1 hour long and cover a complete business case simulation with detailed feedback and advice. The rates we charge are much lower than the average market rates and all proceeds go to charity!

Prep Guides

We're in the process of developing a number of case interview related e-guides that we send to our followers in exchange for a small charity donation. Subscribe to our newsletter in order to stay in touch and look out for freebies in the future!
